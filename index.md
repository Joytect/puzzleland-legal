# Privacy Policy for Puzzle Land

**Last Updated:** March 5, 2026

Puzzle Land ("we", "our", or "the app") is a jigsaw puzzle application suitable for all ages. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored On-Device Only
- **Puzzle Progress:** Completed puzzles, stars earned, and best times
- **App Settings:** Sound preferences
- **Parental Access PIN:** If set, a 4-digit PIN used to restrict access to the Settings screen
- **Unlocked Content:** Which puzzles and categories are unlocked

All of this data is stored **locally on your device only** and is never transmitted to our servers.

### Information Collected by Third-Party SDKs

#### Advertising (Start.io)
Puzzle Land displays ads through Start.io. The SDK is configured with COPPA compliance and Mixed Audience mode, which limits data collection. Start.io may collect:
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

For details, see [Start.io Privacy Policy](https://www.start.io/policy/privacy-policy-site).

#### Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)
We use Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix app crashes. Crashlytics collects:
- Crash logs and stack traces
- Device model and OS version
- App state at the time of crash

No personally identifiable information is collected through Crashlytics. For details, see [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

## How We Use Information

- To save your puzzle progress locally on your device
- To display age-appropriate advertisements
- To detect and fix crashes and bugs

## Data Storage

**All user data is stored locally on your device.** We do not operate any servers and do not store any user data in the cloud. The only network activity is:
- Loading advertisements (Start.io)
- Sending crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

## Parental Access (Parent Gate)

Puzzle Land includes a Parental Access feature to help parents control access to the app's Settings screen:
- Parents can create a 4-digit PIN to protect access to settings
- The PIN is stored **locally on your device only** and is never transmitted to any server
- The PIN can be deleted at any time from the Settings screen
- No personal information is collected as part of this feature

## Privacy Protections

Puzzle Land is suitable for all ages, including young children under parental supervision. We are committed to protecting user privacy:
- We do not collect personal information from users
- All puzzle progress and settings remain on the device
- Ads are configured with child-safe settings (COPPA compliant, Mixed Audience)
- The app does not use behavioral profiling
- No user accounts or registration required

## Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access**: You may request information about data collected through third-party SDKs
- **Right to Erasure**: You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app
- **Right to Object**: You may object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **joytect25@gmail.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

## Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. **Uninstalling the app removes all data** including puzzle progress and sound settings.

## Data Security

All network communications use encrypted connections (HTTPS). No personal data is stored on external servers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
