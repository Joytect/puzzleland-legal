# Data Deletion

**Puzzle Land**: Jigsaw Puzzles for All Ages

---

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete all your data, simply **uninstall the app**.

This removes:
- All puzzle progress (completed puzzles, stars, times)
- All app settings (sound preferences)
- All unlock status (puzzles and categories)

No data remains on your device after uninstallation. No cloud accounts or server data exist.
